\begin{frm-thm}[Pythagoras]
Let $a$, $b$, and $c$ denote the lengths of the sides of a \emph{right 
triangle}, i.e., of a triangle with one angle equal to $90^\circ$. 
Without loss of generality assume that $a\le b<c$. Then
\[ a^2+b^2=c^2. \]
\end{frm-thm}
